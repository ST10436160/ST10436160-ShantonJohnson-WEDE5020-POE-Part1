Our Projects

Green Earth Initiative runs a variety of projects aimed at promoting environmental sustainability. Here are some of our key initiatives:

Community Tree Planting

Our tree planting events engage local communities in reforestation efforts, helping to restore natural habitats and combat climate change.

Environmental Education Workshops

We conduct workshops in schools and communities to educate people about the importance of environmental conservation and how they can contribute.

Clean-Up Drives

Our clean-up drives aim to reduce pollution in local areas, encouraging people to keep their surroundings clean and green.

Advocacy for Policy Change

We work with local governments and NGOs to advocate for policies that support environmental protection and sustainability.
